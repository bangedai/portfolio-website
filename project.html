About Me

Hi there! I’m Prabhat kadel, a data analyst who’s as excited about numbers as most people are about puppies. I find joy in wrangling messy data, uncovering hidden patterns, and weaving those insights into stories that drive smarter decisions. I got my start in finance and discovered that data has this magical way of turning gut feelings into clear, confident strategies. Over the past few years, I’ve honed my skills in SQL, Excel, and Power BI—transforming data chaos into sleek dashboards and actionable recommendations. What excites me most? Finding those “aha!” moments—the kind where the data suddenly clicks, and everything makes sense. I’m currently on the lookout for new challenges and opportunities to collaborate with like-minded teams. When I’m not glued to my computer, I’m probably out hiking, getting lost in a podcast, or experimenting with new coffee brews. Let’s connect and turn data into impact together!
